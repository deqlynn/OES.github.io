SOALAN LAZIM



1. What is iCerdik?

iCerdik Program™ is an interactive learning program developed to help students be exam ready and score straight A's. Our program also provides parents with a simple tool to systematically monitor their children's academic progress, thus allowing them to assist their children in preparing for examinations.

iCerdik Program™ trains students how to apply what they have studied in school through specially prepared assessments and revision exercises.

2. What is 'Input Learning'?

'Input Learning' is putting information into your brain. Reading, studying, listening and memorising are examples of 'Input Learning'. Too many students today only carry out 'Input Learning', that is why they get average marks. For students to excel, they need both 'Input Learning' and iCerdik Program™.

3. Why do students need iCerdik?

iCerdik Program™ has been carefully designed to provide students with many features to help them prepare for their examinations. With iCerdik Program™, students will be able to assess their level of understanding of all subjects or topics that they have been taught in school. iCerdik Program™ also allows students unlimited practices to sharpen their examination skills, helps build their confidence, and encourages them to develop a iCerdik attitude.

4. What are the levels and subjects covered by iCerdik?

iCerdik Program™ covers all subjects taught in National schools from Year 1 to Form 3. The contents of iCerdik Program™ is in line with the school syllabus and curriculum set by the Ministry of Education, and covers all major government examinations - UPSR & PMR.

5. Why is the 'Performance Report' in iCerdik important?

'Performance Report' is an important feature that gives parents unprecedented first hand knowledge of their children's academic performances. Parents will be well informed of their children's strong and weak subjects even before their children bring home their actual school report card.

'Performance Report' enables parents to monitor their children's progress at any time convenient to them. Parents then can render assistance to their children, if and when necessary.

6. How will I know if my children are doing well in iCerdik system?

As parents you are able to review your children's progress and performances by looking at their 'Performance Report'. The Performance Report is a 'must have' feature for parents as it provides the much-needed feedback for parents to make decisions.

7. How can I monitor my children's progress, as I do not have the time to constantly sit with him through the lessons?

iCerdik Program™ is designed for today's busy parents. iCerdik Program™ provides parents with the right tools and much needed features to help parents simplify their task of monitoring their children's academic progress in a much more easier, systematic, efficient and effective manner. You can view your child's 'Performance Report' real-time, at your convenience, at anytime, and anywhere.

8. How do you motivate students to continue practising on your iCerdik?

Our interactive features are one of the prime motivator that encourages students to continue practising their revision lessons and test sessions on iCerdik Program™. In addition, children love to compete. That is why iCerdik Program™ will grade and list 100 best students in all subjects and at every level. Students will be motivated if they see their names on the top 100 list and they will strive to further improve their rankings. The more they practice; the better will be their performance.

9. Why does iCerdik focus only on objective-type questions?

This is because iCerdik Program™ applies learning techniques that tests students on their level of understanding through multiple-choice questions. The mastering of multiple-choice questions will enable students to do well in the examinations that are based on subjective-type questions.

10. Why are workings, especially for Mathematics, not shown?

iCerdik Program™ is not 'Input Learning'. 'Input Learning' places great emphasis on teaching methods, which the students have already undergone in schools, tuition classes and/or learnt from reference books. iCerdik Program™ allows students the flexibility to adopt the 'Input Learning' methods that best suits them. The unique strength of iCerdik Program™ is based on the simple but crucial fact that different students learn in different ways; what seems simple to one student may be difficult for another. iCerdik Program™ have created and developed a learning method that systematically identifies students' weaknesses, enabling them to take remedial steps to improve while enjoying self-paced lessons, preparing them to be exam-ready and helping them reach academic excellence.
